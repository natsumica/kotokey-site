Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KotoKey (“the App”) handles user information.

1. Information We Store

The App may store information necessary to provide its features on your device, such as app settings, keyboard themes, typing sounds, user dictionary entries, and recently used emoji.

2. Keyboard Input

KotoKey does not send the text you type with the keyboard to external servers. Input is used only to provide keyboard functionality and local suggestions on your device.

3. Full Access

The App may require Full Access in order to reflect settings from the main app to the keyboard extension, such as themes, typing sounds, and user dictionary entries. Basic keyboard input features are available even without Full Access.

4. Advertising

The App may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect information such as advertising identifiers, device information, and usage data for ad delivery.

5. In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases using Apple StoreKit. Purchase processing is handled by Apple.

6. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not provide personal information to third parties except as required by law.

8. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. Changes become effective when posted on this page.
